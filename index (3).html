About Us

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is widely recognized as one of the premier secondary schools in the region. As a DepEd-accredited science high school, it offers a rigorous and specialized curriculum focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. This challenging program attracts and develops students of exceptional intelligence, nurturing their critical thinking, creativity, and passion for learning. Beyond academics, BCNSHS also instills discipline, responsibility, and integrity among its students, ensuring they grow into well-rounded individuals.
